I created (Proz)ente after I made the German word "Ente" (duck) out of the German word "Prozente" (precents)

How to play

(Proz)ente is a game of words (and ducks). There are 2 teams (or 2 players). The first team/player writes a word on a paper, draws a duck on the paper, gives the paper to the second team/player, and starts a timer for 30 seconds. The second team/player also writes a word, but they can only reorganize the letters of the first team/player's word and/or remove letters, without adding any more letters. If they can do it within the 30 seconds, they get a point. No? The first team gets a point. They switch positions (first team/player writes <-> second team/player writes) and the game continues. The team/player with the most points at the end of the game wins!
